Lemon Pie

Description

Fresh Lemon Pie recipe is a refreshing dessert with a creamy filling full of lemon flavor! Sour cream lemon pie in a graham cracker crust.

It’s no wonder that Lemon Meringue Pie and Key Lime Pie are some of the most popular Fruit Pie Recipes. The cool, creamy filling and tons of fresh flavors from real fruit make the perfect Summer Dessert!

The sweet Graham Cracker Crust, creamy lemon filling, and fluffy Whipped Cream topping make this dessert irresistible. Ingredients

  • Graham Cracker Crust
  • Condensed Milk
  • Lemon Juice
  • Sour Cream
  • Whipped Cream

Steps

Time needed: 2 hours and 20 minutes

  1. Make the Crust
    2. Preheat your oven. Combine the graham cracker crumbs with the butter and sugar until they have a wet sand consistency.
  2. Bake the Crust
    3. Press the crust mixture into an 8-inch pie dish. Bake for 7 minutes.
  3. Mix Lemon Filling
    4. While the crust is baking, combine the condensed milk, sour cream, and lemon juice in a large bowl. Whisk until completely smooth.
  4. Fill Crust
    5. After baking, allow the crust to cool for a few minutes then pour the filling into the baked crust.
  5. Bake the Filling
    6. Return the filled crust to the oven and bake for an additional 5-8 minutes. You are looking for tiny bubbles just beginning to form. Keep an eye on it because you don’t want to let the pie brown at all.
  6. Fill to Set
    7. Cool the pie for a bit, then refrigerate uncovered for at least 2 hours, or until the filling is completely set.
  7. Decorate and Serve
    8. Pipe whipped cream around the edge of the set pie and serve. Keep the pie chilled when not serving.
